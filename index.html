Farm Biosecurity Guidelines

Maintain Hygiene

Keep the farm clean and sanitized to prevent spread of infections.

Regular Vaccination

Ensure timely vaccination and deworming of animals to stay disease-free.

Visitor Control

Restrict and monitor visitor access to maintain biosecurity standards.

Health Monitoring

Check livestock health regularly and report any unusual symptoms quickly.

Waste Management

Dispose of farm waste responsibly to avoid contamination risks.

Emergency Preparedness

Have a response plan for sudden outbreaks and farm emergencies.
